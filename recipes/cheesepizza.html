Cheese Pizza

Cheese Pizza is a classic. Whether you enjoyed it from the box in the freezer section as a kid or had a favorite pizzeria to order from, most of us have a core Cheese Pizza memory.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 475°.
  2. Prepare dough according to recipe directions. Grease baking pans. Divide dough into three or four portions and stretch to fit into pan.
  3. Spread ½ to ⅔ cup of sauce onto each pizza round. Top with cheese.
  4. Bake 10 – 12 minutes or until crust is crisp and cheese is gooey and golden.
